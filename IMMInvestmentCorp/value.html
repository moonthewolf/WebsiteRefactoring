  • Focus on small-to-mid cap enterprises

    IMM Investment plans to primarily focus on small-to-medium sized companies. Transaction value in this segment is typically in the USD 1m ~ USD 50mn range. IMM Investment has been discovering and investing businesses that have potential to build global competitiveness with Its own technology, marketing strategy and executive ability.

  • Balanced portfolio

    IMM Investment has built well-balanced portfolio of IT, Healthcare/Biotech and Manufacturing. Every investment professionals evaluates a potential of candidate companies with its own experiences and insight on specialized industry. Through its balanced portfolio, IMM Investment manages risk of specific industry effectively.

  • Value-up activity

    IMM Investment has experience significantly increasing value of our portfolio companies by driving positive changes in business strategy, customer acquisition. cost rationalization, balance sheet restructuring and exit planning. IMM Investment actively manages our portfolio companies to help them grow and manage risk effectively through our representation at the board and/or management level of out portfolio companies.
