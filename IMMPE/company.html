  • IMM Private Equity consists of partners and investment professionals with deep investment experience in Korea. With our expertise and experience, IMM Private Equity has a strong track record in both buyout and growth capital investments.
  • IMM Private Equity plays a vital role in helping its portfolio companies reach their full growth potential by providing long-term capital and operational expertise.
  • IMM Private Equity cooperates with the management teams of the portfolio companies to bring about positive changes in key areas such as revenue growth, employment increase, cost optimization, efficient supply chain management, overseas expansion and tailored incentive programs.

A Leading Player in the Korean Private Equity Industry

IMM RoseGold I (2008)
IMM Private Equity raised the USD 272mm first flagship fund, IMM Rosegold I, in 2008.
IMM RoseGold II (2012)
IMM Private Equity raised the USD 657mm second flagship fund, IMM Rosegold II, in 2011/12. This fund had the most number of Korean investors in that vintage and was the first fund where we received commitments from international investors.
IMM RoseGold III (2016)
IMM Private Equity raised the USD 1.16bn third flagship fund, IMM Rosegold III, in 2015/2016. This was again the fund with the most number of Korean investors in that vintage. We also received significantly more commitments from our international investors.
